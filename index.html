Left unsupervised, he overthinks, misses you, stares at old chats, and becomes dramatically sad.

Reason #8

Because You're His Favorite Person 🌹

The person he wants to tell everything to. The person he thinks about first. The person he hopes stays.

Reason #9

Because He Knows He Was Wrong 😔

He's afraid, not of losing an argument..... but of losing you.

Reason #10

Because He Wants One More Chance ❤️

Not because he deserves it. But because he'll spend every day trying to earn it.
